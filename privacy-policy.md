# Privacy Policy – StickerDrop

**Last updated: March 2026**

StickerDrop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

## Permissions used

- **storage**: Caches sticker images locally on your device. No personal data is stored.
- **activeTab**: Detects the chat input field to paste stickers. No page content is read or transmitted.
- **scripting**: Injects the sticker picker UI into supported chat pages only.
- **host permissions**: Accesses chat sites to inject UI, and GitHub to fetch sticker images.

## Third parties

No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.
